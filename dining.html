Cape Cod has wonderful, delicious dining options to satisfy your culinary pleasures. There are family and waterfront restaurants, bars and taverns, bakeries, cafes, and of course - a summertime must - ice cream parlors.

My favorite restaurant in South Yarmouth, Cape Cod is Skipper Restaurant and Chowder House after a day at the beach. Their clam chowder is award-winning, delicious, and a favorite of my daughter's. They have fried seafood,broiled seafood, lobster, clams, mussels, shrimps, everything to satisfy a seafood lover. They are open for lunch and dinner and located near Parker River Beach, a favorite beach for children and families
