    • Cape Cod, Massachussettes

    My favorite vacation spot in the USA!

    Cape Cod, Massachusettes is made up of about 20 beautiful towns and the islands of Martha's Bay, Nantucked, and Monomy. It is a very popular vaction spot for Americans as well as people from countries around the world. Cape Cod is famous for it's delicious seafood restaurants, country style ice cream parlors, and of course, its gorgeous beaches and light- houses. It's a wonderful place to bring your family, your romantic partner, or even yourself, if you so desire. There are many activities for fun such as water sports, swimming, surfing, sailing, cruises, and mini-golf. And if you want to go to relax and enjoy beautiful sunlit beaches and sunsets with a glass of wine, it is divine. I have been going there for years, and can honestly say it is my "heaven on earth".
