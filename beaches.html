Cape Cod has beautiful beaches in each town. You can visit beaches on the bay side or the ocean side.

Cape Cod has 559.6 miles of unspoiled coastline, and many beautiful sandy beaches for you to enjoy. The Cape Cod National Seashore also has 40 miles of sandy beach in Outer Cape Cod. Each town has a variety of beaches which are family friendly, good for surfing and water sports, and which provide a relaxing haven for us sunbathing lovers. You can find information about any of them at the Cape Cod Chamber of Commerce, https://www.capecodchamber.org
